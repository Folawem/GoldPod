Our Services

Expert Custom Tailoring

Our custom sewing services are designed to bring your vision to life. Whether you're looking for traditional Nigerian attire or modern fashion, our master tailor delivers impeccable craftsmanship.

We specialize in:

  • Agbada: The majestic and flowing Agbada, perfect for special occasions, crafted with precision.
  • Buba and Sokoto: Comfortable and stylish traditional wear, tailored to fit you perfectly.
  • Iro and Buba: Elegance and tradition come together in these beautifully crafted garments.
  • Ankara: Vibrant and versatile, Ankara fabrics are transformed into stunning outfits.
  • Kaftan: A symbol of grace, the Kaftan is tailored to enhance your natural beauty.

Our tailor's expertise ensures that each piece is a perfect blend of tradition, style, and personal preference.

Precision Fabric Cutting

Our cutting services ensure that your fabrics are prepared with the utmost precision. We use modern equipment and techniques to deliver cuts that meet your exact specifications.

Our cutting services include:

  • Custom cutting based on your design needs.
  • High-precision cuts for intricate designs.
  • Quick turnaround times for bulk orders.

Whether you need fabrics cut for a simple dress or a complex design, our services are tailored to meet your needs.

Fabric Patterns

At GoldPod, we offer a variety of fabric patterns to suit every style and occasion:

  • Lace: Known for its intricate and elaborate designs, perfect for special events.
  • Atiku: Features subtle, understated patterns for a more refined look.
  • Wool: Minimalist in design but rich in texture and bold in color, wool is perfect for classic, elegant styles.

Explore our range of fabrics and find the perfect pattern for your next outfit.
